MinIO — Prometheus Observability Fix
60,400 ⭐ · Production-grade distributed object storage · Used by Netflix, Dropbox, Cloudflare
Identified that ttfb_seconds_distribution was incorrectly typed as a gauge, making it incompatible with Prometheus' histogram_quantile() — the function every SRE team relies on for p50/p95/p99 latency SLOs. Changed metric type to histogram, updated the descriptor accordingly. Reviewed and approved by harshavardhana (MinIO's lead maintainer) and merged into master in April 2025, then cherry-picked into the stable release.
